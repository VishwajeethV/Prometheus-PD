Technology Highlights

N₂O Hybrid Engine

Food-grade nitrous oxide — no DRDO controls, no hazmat licensing. Helium pressurisation prevents self-chilling.

Carbon Fibre Integration

Filament-wound construction. One machine produces motor case, oxidizer tank, and airframe tube.

Dual-Redundant Recovery

Capsule and booster recovered separately. Drogue at 10 km, main at 3 km. Crew Escape System valid to 50 km.

Active Guidance

4 electric-actuated fins. 100 Hz PID control from liftoff. Zero roll imparted to capsule.

Premium Capsule

3.6 m³ pressurised volume. 600mm panoramic dome window. Dual-redundant avionics.

CEA-Verified Design

Thermodynamic & ballistic modeling. Burn chamber tested & validated.
