Why VH4 Aerospace exists

Prometheus-1 provides domestic access to suborbital flight in India. The deck documents a gap: India has 3.5 lakh+ dollar millionaires and zero domestic operators. IN-SPACe policy enables private launch — VH4 intends to be the first-mover using a fully Indian-designed suborbital vehicle.

Nearest seat is abroad

Indian HNWIs face foreign exchange exposure, visas, and long travel. No rupee-denominated option exists.

No domestic microgravity platform

ISRO sounding rockets are not commercially available for private payloads; payloads face long queues and cost.

First-mover window

IN-SPACe opened the door — the first-mover window is open and unclaimed.

Prometheus-1

A fully Indian-designed, Indian-built suborbital vehicle. One passenger. 120 km apogee. 2.7 minutes weightless. Recovered and reflown within 2 weeks.
